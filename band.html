Journey

Band Members

  • Neal Schon: all guitars, backing vocals
  • Ross Valory: bass, backing vocals
  • Jonathan Cain: keyboards, backing vocals
  • Steve Smith: drums
  • Arnel Pineda: lead vocals

Albums

  • Eclipse: newest album with brand new materials released in 2011. Features 12 tracks.
  • Revelation: released in 2008, included a DVD of Las Vegas concert marking the first U.S. public appearance of Journey's current lineup in 2008.
  • Generations: released in 2005, is a second full studio album with lead singer Steve Augeri and drummer Deen Castronovo.

To find out more about Journey, visit their website at: www.journeymusic.com
